Our Cozumel dive courses are available, across all levels. From absolute beginner divers to professional divers, you will learn under the careful care of our highly skilled and experienced SSI Certified Dive Instructors. A variety of courses and advice are available to suit your needs. As a certified diver we help you to maximize your skills and adventure level, while upgrading your certificate level.

Our Scuba Dive Courses go beyond all other Dive Operators, to give you the most interactive fun filled learning and adventure scuba diving excursion available, discovering the most magnificent #1 Coral Reefs right here in the Caribbean Sea of Cozumel Mexico.

Our courses are offered on a private or semi-private basis, fully tailored to the most unique vacation schedules. Our Goal at Dive Galaxsea is to create a competent, knowledgeable Diver. Our Scuba Diving in Cozumel consist of, private diving instruction, rental gear, dive tanks, diving weights, BCD, Dive Regulator, Diving Mask, Dive Fins, Snorkel, wet suit (if needed), Scuba Diving Certification fees, Personal Log Book, Diving Text Book, and RDP (Dive Planner). With Absolutely NO HIDDEN FEES. If you select to use E-Learning there will be an additional charge of $65 on your Cozumel Open Water Course. Come Dive Cozumel, and see for yourself what the excitement is about when diving with Dive GalaxSea!
