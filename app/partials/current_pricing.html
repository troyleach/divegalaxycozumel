For a detail description of each Cozumel Coral Reef site and Dive Site, check out our Interactive Reef and Dive Site Map.

Cozumel reefs and shallow coral formations that extend from the southern tip of the magnificent island to just south of the art filled, unique and friendly San Miguel on the west coast is fantastic for Snorkeling Tours and Scuba Diving Tours. Two Main Coral Reef systems surround Cozumel Island, which are Colombia reef and Palancar reef easily reachable starting at around 30 feet (9 meters). Chankanaabs Lagoon offers Cozumel snorkelers and Cozumel scuba divers a much more accessible coral and limestone formation approximately as shallow as 6- 30 feet (2-9 meters).
